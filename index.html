About Us

Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Limited (TNPL) is one of India's leading manufacturers of eco-friendly paper products. Established in 1979, our Kagithapuram plant in Karur District produces high-quality newsprint and printing & writing paper using sustainable practices.

Sustainability Initiatives

TNPL is committed to environmentally responsible manufacturing with initiatives including:

  • 100% utilization of agro-residues as raw material
  • Extensive plantation program (over 100 million saplings planted)
  • Comprehensive water recycling systems
  • Renewable energy generation
